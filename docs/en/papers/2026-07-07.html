Lead Story
tencent/Hy3: 295B MoE Model Released Under Apache 2.0

Tencent's Hy team releases Hy3, a 295B-parameter Mixture-of-Experts model with 21B active parameters and 3.8B MTP layer parameters. After incorporating feedback from 50+ products and scaling up post-training data, the model is now open-sourced under Apache 2.0 license, suitable for diverse applications.
