Scientists reveal what really happens when water is trapped in tiny spaces ScienceDaily 科学

A decades-old puzzle about water is solved: water trapped in nanoscale spaces is not inherently more reactive; intense pressures inside microscopic gaps explain most effects, while surrounding material can further enhance chemistry if it interacts with reaction products.
