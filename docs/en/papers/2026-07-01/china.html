China Watch: CPC 105th Anniversary Grand Ceremony Held; Various Social Developments

On July 1, the grand ceremony marking the 105th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China was held in Beijing. General Secretary Xi Jinping delivered an important speech, emphasizing staying true to the original aspiration and keeping the mission firmly in mind, and striving unitedly to achieve the second centenary goal. The China Earthquake Administration released the intensity map of the 5.5-magnitude earthquake in Gaoxian County, Sichuan. Socially, the 2026 summer travel season kicked off, with China Railway Shenyang Bureau expecting to transport 46.5 million passengers; Helingeer New District in Inner Mongolia transformed from a small station into 'China Cloud Valley'; community healthcare reforms in Ningbo's Siming Mountain area are retaining doctors; five rivers in Guangxi experienced floods above warning levels.
