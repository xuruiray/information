NASA's Lucy spacecraft discovered that asteroid Donaldjohanson is a wobbling, peanut-shaped relic born from a violent collision and slowly reshaped by the subtle force of sunlight. It also carries traces of ancient water, making it an important clue to the solar system's mysterious past.

A shortage of vitamin B12 can have major effects on health and energy. First linked to pernicious anemia treatment a century ago, new research shows B12 helps maintain mitochondrial function, which may explain why deficiency mimics normal aging.

Researchers found that a rare liver cancer evades immunotherapy by luring immune T cells away from the tumor and trapping them in nearby fibrous tissue. The FDA-approved drug AMD3100 freed those T cells to attack the cancer, significantly improving immunotherapy effectiveness in tumor samples.
