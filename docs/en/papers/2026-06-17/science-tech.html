A new SETI study suggests we may be overlooking alien signals not because they aren't there, but because their own stars are scrambling them before escaping. Turbulent plasma and stellar storms spread ultra-narrow transmissions across frequencies, complicating detection.

A new study finds chronic wasting disease can spread silently with infectious prions in asymptomatic animals. Though no confirmed human risk, the disease's ability to evolve and cross species warrants close attention.
