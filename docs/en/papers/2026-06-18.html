GLM-5.2: Most powerful text-only open weights LLM released

Chinese AI lab Z.ai released GLM-5.2 on June 16, a 753B-parameter Mixture-of-Experts model with 40B active parameters and 1.51TB weights, under MIT license. Text-only input, it is considered the most powerful open weights LLM for text tasks.
