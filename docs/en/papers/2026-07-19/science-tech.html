Scientists built a camera that can track invisible particles in 3D ScienceDaily 科学

New particle detector PLATON replaces millions of tiny components with a single light-producing block. Using a light-field camera, sensitive photon sensors and AI, it reconstructs particle paths in fast 3D. Simulations suggest it could match or surpass today's best detectors while being far easier to scale.
