China Watch Briefing

Today's China Watch covers two major subsections: Policy & Regulation, and Society & Law. On the policy front, several officials from Shanxi, Guizhou, Henan and other provinces have been investigated or expelled from the Party for disciplinary violations, including Du Xinjian (former deputy general manager of Jincheng Coal Group), Zhou Tao (former inspector of Shanxi Justice Department), Ding Zhixue (former director of Guizhou People's Congress Financial Committee), and Tian Lin (deputy director of Zhoukou People's Congress). On the society front, organic vegetables gain popularity, Beijing releases the 2026 points-based household registration list, two typhoons – Bavi and Haishen – affect China's seas and northeastern regions, and Shenyang raises its flood control emergency response to Level 1.
