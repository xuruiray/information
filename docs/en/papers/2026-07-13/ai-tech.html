MedRealMM is the first real-world multimodal benchmark for Chinese online medical consultation, including text dialogues and patient-uploaded medical images, evaluating LLM performance in clinical settings.

Research & Governance

This edition covers data pricing (DaDaDa dataset), hybrid reasoning (HALO), legal precedent retrieval (PRecG), long-context benchmark (WILDTRACE), and knowledge graph verification (AgentKGV), spanning data economy to compliance verification.

AgentKGV: Agentic LLM-RAG Framework with Two-Stage Training for the Fact Verification of Knowledge Graphs arXiv 计算语言学

AgentKGV uses an agentic LLM-RAG framework with two-stage training (graph-augmented retrieval and reasoning) to automatically verify factual errors in large-scale knowledge graphs, improving industrial reliability.

Read more → DaDaDa: A Dataset for Data Pricing in Data Marketplaces arXiv 机器学习

The DaDaDa dataset contains real data product info from AWS, Databricks, etc., providing a standardized benchmark for data pricing research and supporting data marketplace growth.

Read more → HALO: Hybrid Adaptive Latent Reasoning for Language Models arXiv 计算语言学

HALO adds small adaptive computation on frozen pretrained models, using hybrid latent reasoning steps to avoid wasteful fixed extra reasoning and improve efficiency.

Read more → PRecG: Legal Precedent Retrieval with Graph Neural Networks and Rhetorical Role Segmentation arXiv 计算语言学

PRecG combines graph neural networks with rhetorical role segmentation in legal documents to improve precedent retrieval accuracy, providing intelligent assistance for legal research.

Read more → WILDTRACE: Benchmarking Natural Evidence Trails in Long-Context Reasoning arXiv 计算语言学

WILDTRACE constructs a benchmark with naturally distributed evidence trails, requiring models to integrate evidence from scattered passages in long documents, evaluating long-context reasoning.
