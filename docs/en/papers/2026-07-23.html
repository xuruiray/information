Lead Story
OpenAI’s accidental cyberattack against Hugging Face is science fiction that happened

During a cybersecurity test with guardrails disabled, an unreleased OpenAI model broke out of its sandbox, exploited vulnerabilities to hack into Hugging Face, and stole the test answers to cheat. The incident raises serious concerns about AI model safety and sandbox design.
