Ornith-1.0: Self-Scaffolding LLMs for Agentic Coding

DeepReinforce releases Ornith-1.0, the first self-scaffolding open-source model series built on Gemma 4 and Qwen 3.5, ranging from 9B to 397B parameters, achieving state-of-the-art performance among open-source models for agentic coding tasks.
